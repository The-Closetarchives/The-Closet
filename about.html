About

THE CLOSET is a digital archive dedicated to the exploration of disturbing histories, unexplained events, folklore, legends, and psychological horror.

Our work focuses on researching real-world mysteries, historical records, documented events, witness accounts, and evolving theories from multiple sources in order to present topics with both atmosphere and accuracy. While some stories remain unresolved, every effort is made to separate confirmed information from speculation wherever possible.Research for THE CLOSET is gathered through archived records, historical references, documented reports, public sources, articles, translated materials, and evolving discussions surrounding each subject. Whenever possible, topics are approached through multiple perspectives in order to preserve both the facts and the mysteries that continue to surround them.

Beyond research-based archives, THE CLOSET also preserves original fictional works within the Fictional Hall - a growing collection of dark fiction, psychological horror, and strange narratives created to expand the world beyond reality.

As the archive continues to grow, future projects may include community submissions, monthly creative contests, and collaborative storytelling experiences for visitors drawn toward the unknown.

Some histories are forgotten. Others refuse to disappear.
